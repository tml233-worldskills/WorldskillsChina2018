The Associated General Contractors of America (AGC) and the Associated Schools of Construction (ASC) support building a talented work force through the U.S. National Student Competition. Teams are critiqued on estimating, bidding, planning, scheduling, presentation skills, creativity, understanding of sound construction techniques and thoughtful methodology throughout a two¨Cday simulated construction project. Students present both oral and written presentations to a panel of judges, and first, second and third place awards are given for each division.
